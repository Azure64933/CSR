tors, , the Company allocated resources and spare time on drafting the sustainability strategy step by step. Such strategy had been fully discussed by man-
agement so that the business development and related performances of the Company can be better aligned with the defined sustainability targets and 
the strategy can be implemented among different divisions in a harmonized and cooperated way. Finally, the administration department reported annual 
performances in this CSR report accordance with material topics identified with mans of a systematic analysis.
